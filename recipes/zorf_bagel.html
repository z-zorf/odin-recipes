The Zorf: A Bagel

What are we cooking?

There's nothing better than a delicious bagel sandwich. This recipe will help you up level your ordering so you can maximize your bagel enjoyment!

As a warning, when you order this bagel in a store, it might turn some heads. But don't worry, it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • Any bagel (lightly toasted if not fresh)
  • Tuna salad
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Optional: Dijon Mustard

Steps:

  1. Slice your bagel
  2. Spread light layer of cream cheese
  3. If you're using mustard, add that
  4. Add tuna salad
  5. Add lettuce & tomato
  6. Enjoy!
