Chemex Coffee

What are we cooking?

Chemex coffee makers are a great way to make delicious pour over coffee without limiting yourself to just one cup!

Chemexes were created in 1941, have a really unique design, and make some of the best coffee out there.

Ingredients:

  • Chemex
  • Filter
  • Coffee (ground, not too fine)
  • Hot water (preferably 190F)

Steps:

  1. Heat up chemex with warm water and wet the filter
  2. Place ground coffee in the filter
  3. Heat water to 190F
  4. Pour in circular motion so ground are just wet, allowing it to bubble and for gasses to release
  5. Let all water run out, and fill with 190F water up to nearly the top
  6. Repeat one more time if you need more coffee
  7. Enjoy!
