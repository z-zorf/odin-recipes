Dry Brined Salmon

What are we cooking?

Dry brined salmon is an easy way to make delicious salmon that is immune from over cooking. All it takes is a little time and a lot of salt!

To complete this recipe, all you'll need is salmon filets with the skin on, salt (preferably kosher), oil, and some paper towels.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon (skin on)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare tray with two layers of paper towel
  2. Take your salmon filets and salt them all over
  3. Place in tray skin side up
  4. Let sit for at least 8 hours, up to 2 days
  5. When ready, pat off excess salt, put on some olive oil
  6. Broil under high heat for 10 minutes
